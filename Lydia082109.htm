August 21, 2009, Friday

3 years

Anniversary is always hard and feeling is always mixed.

Hard? Yes.  But we are still thankful.

During last few weeks, we have received many cards, emails, phone calls, and donations to Lydia memorial fund, from friends and brothers and sisters.  God really showed us His ways to comfort us.

“Do not be anxious about anything, but in everything, by prayer and petition, with thanksgiving, present your requests to God.  And the peace of God, which transcends all understanding, will guard your hears and your minds in Christ Jesus” Philippians 4:6,7

 

There is a coincidence that Esther also left for college this morning, on August 21.

Friends have been asking me last few months if I am ready to let go of Esther for college.  Well, compared with my other daughter, Lydia’s departure 3 years ago, Esther’s departure is easy. 

No problem. 

Praise the Lord.
