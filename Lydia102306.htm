October 23, 2006, Monday

It’s the end of the first quarter of the school.  All three girls did not have school today.  So I took off from work as well.  Not a very good idea.  I think I got too much free time to think about Lydia.  I cried a lot today, while I was alone.  It’s really a very sorrowful day for me.  A neighbor saw me in the evening mentioned that I looked not well.  That reminded me that I need to stick around with mom and girls more, not by myself.

 

Priscilla received a gift card from the school staffs after Lydia passed away.  It was for the Build-A-Bear gift.  Priscilla held it for a while, not sure what to do.  Today, she decided to build a bear, for…, Esther.  Yes, Esther.  Lydia had quite a few bears from birthday parties.  Deborah had one.  Priscilla did not have any until Lydia suggested to buy her one on Priscilla’s birthday.  Esther never got one.  (I thought she was too old for this kind of toy.)  However, when Priscilla gave to Esther, Esther was so happy.  Mom and dad were happy that Priscilla got Lydia’s spirits, willing to share...

 

We started to hear some exciting and interesting stories about Lydia’s bracelet.  It’s comforting to know Lydia’s life continue to be told and shared.  Through Lydia’s faith, love of the Lord and people, may God’s name be glorified.
