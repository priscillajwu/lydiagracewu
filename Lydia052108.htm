May 21, 2008, Wednesday

21 months, Lydia left us…

 

Saw and heard so much about earthquake in Suchuan, China, it was not easy for me at all this past week.

Seeing so many parents lost their ONLY child in the earthquake, my heart aches and aches.

My brain could not comprehend.

I saw a picture of a young father, sat on top of the rubble of the school building, sorted through his deceased child’s school back bag with a lot of tears on his face.

Oh, almighty God, please have mercy.

We, the human being are so helpless.

Please have mercy on us.

And yes, God is always merciful and full of grace.

Continue trust and obey…
