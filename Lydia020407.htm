February 4, 2007, Sunday

The day for the XLI Super Bowl.

Last year, February 5, 2006, like millions of other American families, we watched part of the game with Lydia suffered her headache (we thought it was flu), without knowing the big storm, the worse nightmare a parent could ever experience, was coming to hit us the very next day…

Tonight, while still remembered that the Rolling Stone performed in the half time show, we sat in front of TV to watch the game, of course, without Lydia on the couch.

 

At the end of game tonight, the winner Indianapolis Colts’s owner and coach Dungy gave all the glories to the Lord, our God, in front of countless TV viewers.

What hit me the most was that coach Dungy also lost his son recently (to a suicide over a year ago).  He came back with the still strong Christian faith and won the Super Bowl championship.  That is truly amazing.

I simply kept my everyday life going.  I thought that is difficult enough.  But Dungy could win Super Bowl.  Wow, I felt bad about myself.

 

Still, thank God for the great inspiration.
