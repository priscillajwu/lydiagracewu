Last year, Lydia’s Memorial Service was on scheduled musical performance day.  As we know, Lydia always enjoyed musical theater experiences. This year, we will like to remember her by performing church children musical called Living It All (Living it out in this upside down world) to reach out more people in our community about Christ the Lord. You and your family are cordially invited to enjoy an evening of music and performance by children from Cincinnati Chinese Church and some of Lydia’s friends. And message from our youth pastor.

We also plan to show a video clip about Lydia’s end days – how she never lost hope, still lives for Christ.

 

Please come and celebrate Lydia’s life with us!

Welcome to send your memory of Lydia to us, we treasure them!
