January 22, 2007, Monday

Mom met a friend today.  Totally insensitive about what mom was feeling, this friend kept bragging about her own children.  How good her own children are doing…

I guess a lot of moms were like that.  Their children are their everything, their ultimate dreams.  I don’t blame her.  Just felt a bit cruel.

 

In Genesis 37:35, after Jacob thought Joseph had died.

All his sons and daughters came to comfort him, but he refused to be comforted. “No,” he said, “in mourning will I go down to the grave to my son.”

 

This is so real and so true.  Mourning is real.  We need to know earthly dreams will shatter, just a matter of time.  Nobody can be immune.

Where can the comfort come from?  From people?  So limited and so short?  How long can it last?

Only the Lord is the true comfort.
