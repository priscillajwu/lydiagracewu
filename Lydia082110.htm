August 21, 2010, Saturday

4 years

Even after four years.  We were amazed that we received quite a few cards, emails, and many greeted us in person and mentioned to us about Lydia’s anniversary.  Probably a lot more wanted to mentioned it but decided not to.

It meant a lot to us.  Thanks for doing so and we thank God for not forgetting us and Lydia.  He sent us the comfort on this anniversary and Lydia’s birthday two weeks earlier, through many of you.

Lydia’s same age friends are going to high school now.

I often looked at them and tried to imagine how tall Lydia would be, and what she may look like at the age of 14.

Well, probably not very constructive, but a father’s dream to see how his departed daughter would look like if she grows up like regular girls.
