October 18, 2006, Wednesday

Mom tried to turn her grieving into actions.  (I always admire her.)

Mom has been praying and talking to several people about how to continue sharing Lydia’s story to reach more people.  Like publishing a book about Lydia, and writing a hymn inspired by Lydia… etc.

She has done the first step to order the wristband about Lydia.  It looks very nice, and it caught my eyes right away.

The color red is Lydia’s favorite color, also represents Christ Jesus’s blood.  The color white means our sins can be washed away and be as pure and as white as snow.

The words, “Lydia Grace Wu + Living It ALL + Love FAITH Hope” reminded us Lydia’s spirit and her story.

Esther prepared one page summary about the wristband.

Mom has contacted several places where people can get the wristband.

If you have burden to help us distribute, please let us know.
