Mom gave her Advil and kept watching her. The fever never really got away for the entire day, slightly lower when under the medication.

Mom called the doctor in the afternoon. He wants us continue observing her closely. Check back with him first thing tomorrow morning.

With regular kids, we probably just stay home and rest, but for Lydia, we weren’t sure. According to her doctor, if she is under chemo therapy, with her temperature today, she needs to be admitted into hospital right away.

Well, maybe over the weekend, Lydia just had too much exposure to the crowd.

Wow, how careful do we need to be, it seems never enough…
