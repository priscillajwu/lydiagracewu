December 3, 2006, Sunday

Life is really full of challenge.  Recently, a few dear families in the church are facing very difficult illness.  While for praying for them sincerely, the thoughts that dear brothers and sisters are suffering hurt me a lot (given the situation I am in now).

Today, I was surprised to find out that simply shopping at Walmart (which I have not been for months, although I used to shop there regularly before) would bring back memories that hurt.

Late in the night, I could not sleep and into deep thinking…

Yes, that’s it.  Although life is continuing difficult, we still have hope.  Not the hope that our suffering will end soon, but the hope in heaven that the Lord promised.

I will trust in the Lord, Christ Jesus, no matter what circumstances…
