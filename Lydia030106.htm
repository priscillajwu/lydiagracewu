March 1, 2006

 

My dear prayer heroes,

 

We met with Children hospital neurosurgeon and oncologists last night.  The last MRI results, taken yesterday morning, after the second surgery, still showed some residual tumor.  The reason is that it was very difficult to identify the tumor with regular brain tissue, even with the help of ultra sound during the operation.

 

I had very complex feelings to hear that.  A bit puzzled….  And again, of course, worries and fears came to attack me again.

 

I prayed and thought it over and over…..  Yes, it has to be the best results in God’s view.  We thought totally clean of tumor is good, but God knows better.

 

Many brothers and sisters, from quite a few churches in Cincinnati, and throughout the nation, south to Florida, west to California, and as far as in Asia, have been praying for Lydia.  The prayer email chains have traveled very far away.  I have received cards, phone calls, and emails from people I do not know.  Many have been keeping Lydia in their prayers.

 

Our God answers our prayer.

 

The physician team in Children hospital remains optimistic about the situation.  They explained, it’s not un-common to have residual for this kind of tumor.  The future treatments, radiation and chemo therapy, should be able to kill the residual tumor.

 

Please continue praying for Lydia, and the physician team planning for the future treatments.

 

Humans are limited.  But God is in control.  We have trust in Him.

 

In Him,

Simon
