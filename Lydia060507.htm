June 5, 2007, Tuesday

Summer swimming season has started.

This year’s swimming team T-shirts changed to the color red, Lydia’s favorite color, in memory of Lydia.

Although we were told that the T-shirts will be made, when I saw it, I was totally caught off guard.  I have to find a place to wipe away my tears.

There are only two sentences, but it’s very hard for me to read through for the first time.  Seeing many kids and swimmers wearing this T-shirt all over the pool area, we felt Lydia’s spirit alive.

Thanks to the Harper’s swim team.

Yes, not only we need to finish the race on earth, but also need to fight a good fight, FOR THE LORD.
