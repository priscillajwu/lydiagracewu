October 6, 2006, Friday

Esther got her driving permit today.

For years, we have been waiting for this day to come.  With four children, we could not wait for Esther to start driving, so the transportation needs can be relieved a little bit.

Lydia would be so happy to see Esther’s driving permit.  She has always been a cheer leader.  Lydia would be so proud of Esther and probably the first to hop into the car while Esther is driving.

 

“Is life more of suffering or more of enjoyment?”  I asked Esther.

“Err…., I would say more of enjoyment,” Esther said, “With suffering, we then enjoy life more.”

 

Tonight, at church, after children program worship, mom shared to all the children,

“This worship DVD, actually Lydia bought it.  She used the gift cards of Christian bookstore she received to buy this DVD, and she wanted to use it in Friday night worship…”  Mom choked up, “Let’s learn from Lydia’s spirit.  Always think of others, love and care for others…”

 

Came home from church.  Usually we would eat some snack and chat with a lot of laughter, about all the joy and fun they had at church and at school.  Again, I felt it’s just too quiet.  I guess mom and all three girls just do not talk as much as Lydia could talk.
