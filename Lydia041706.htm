April 17, 2006, Monday

Another day of radiation.

Out of total of 31 radiation treatments (20 in first part, and 11 in second part), we have finished 24, only seven left to go.  While we are counting, we also recalled many blessings from the Lord.

Many times, we were in fear and despair.  Our Lord has carried us through.  There were so many blessings, hard to count all of them.  He has helped us coming this far, and we believe He will continue to help us.  Although with hardship, His blessings are more apparent and precious.

In today’s regular check up, doctor was happy with Lydia’s progress, except that she needs to eat and drink more…

At night, we tried to feed her.  She just would not take any.  Sip of water was the only thing she could handle.  Ask her why.  She said she was very nauseous, and could not take anything with any flavor or taste.

According to Mom, it’s like a pregnant woman.  I could not imagine what kind of feeling is that.  It must not be very easy to endure.  I pray that she can eat more.  It’s another challenge ahead of us.  She has lost another pound.

Thank you for your prayers.
