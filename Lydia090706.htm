September 7, 2006, Thursday

“I wish I can go water skiing after I get well.”  Lydia said that a few times during her illness.  We regret that Lydia never got chance to do it.  Actually we only took her on vacation once, to a nearby park (one hour away) in June.  And Lydia was very happy during those couple days.  She wrote in her diary, “It’s been a long time since I have so much fun...”

 

“I think Lydia is water skiing right now in heaven.”  Priscilla said to mom today all of sudden.  She probably thought about Lydia.

“Why?”  asked mom.

“She really wanted to do that.” Priscilla continued, “She can do all the fun things in heaven.  I believe there is no school in heaven.  But I think she is still doing the homework, because she enjoyed it.”

Mom laughed, and I laughed pretty loud too when mom told me what Priscilla said.

 

 

“I miss Lydia so much.  You are doing so well.  Let me know how you handle it.”  Mom asked Deborah.

“I miss her a lot too.”  Deborah said, “Lydia got healed.  God healed her so quickly.  She did not need to work so hard to recover.  She was instantly healed….”

 

 

Mom recalled again and again about what Lydia always said to mom,

“Don’t worry, mom.  Jesus will heal me.  I will get better…”

 

 

It’s hard to be alone and looked at Lydia’s pictures.  Too many tears would come out of my eyes too easy, and got my eyes sore and head hurt.  However, it’s also difficult to be with somebody.  I often felt a big lump on my throat when thinking of Lydia when around people.  Without crying, that big lump caused me a lot of discomfort.

In a way, for me, life now seems to be harder than last seven months when Lydia was still fighting the disease.  The pain and sorrow just kept coming.  Without God’s promise and comfort, it would be hard to go on…

 

He will wipe every tear from their eyes. There will be no more death or mourning or crying or pain, for the old order of things has passed away. He who was seated on the throne said, "I am making everything new!" Then he said, "Write this down, for these words are trustworthy and true."

Revelation 21:4,5
