March 28, 2006, Tuesday

I had another sleepless night.  I woke up many times by myself or because Lydia was up.  (She would get up many times a night, most of time going to bathroom, sometimes for pain medicine.)  My mind was troubled with sorrow.  I was awake very early this morning.  I knelt down and prayed.  I was in despair.  Holy spirit inspired me that in life, there are short term sufferings and long term sufferings.  Lydia’s illness seems to be a longer term to me.  However, that does not prevent us from enjoying the life God give us.  Yes, not only peace, we can also have joy.  And that is exactly what God wants us to do. (Philippians 4:4-7)

Yesterday Lydia had loss a lot of hair when she combed her hair.  I put fingers into her hair, and would come out a whole bunch of hair easily (I felt very sad and not quite ready for it.).  Lydia kept doing that seems having a lot of fun.  (Mom gave her a plastic bag to keep the hair.)  Yes, indeed, better than put sorrow into it when hair falling happens.  I think Lydia had learned the lesson ahead of me….
