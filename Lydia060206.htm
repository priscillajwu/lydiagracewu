June 2, 2006, Friday

Lydia went to school to join the field day event. Looking at all the healthy boys and girls running, racing, having fun, wow, it is something.

Lydia tried a few races. She had good time.

On her way out, she noticed all the graduating students had a star (in different colors) hanging from the ceiling of the main aisle in the school. Lydia tried to look for hers and could not find it.

"Is it because I did not come to school?" Lydia was disappointed and said sadly.

"Try to look for red color." Mom said it because some staffs in school knew Lydia’s favorite color is red.

"How could that be?" Lydia said, "It’s in all different colors."

They continued looking for it.

Finally, they found it. It was in the very front of the row, written "Lydia Wu", in color RED.

We realized later that the school staff made that in red on purpose, knowing Lydia’s favorite color is red. Things like this warmed up our hearts so much…

Thank the Lord.
