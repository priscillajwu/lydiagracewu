September 16, 2006, Saturday

We got several free tickets from friends to the Kings Island theme parks today.  Mom and dad did not want to go.  We could not stand to think that only in July, Lydia had so much fun there.  Now, two months later, the theme park is still packed with people, Lydia is no longer with us…

So, we arranged friends to take them.  However, there was change at the last minute.  We had to take the girls.  And they also invited their friends.  At the end, we were one ticket short.  So, Mom and grandma took the girl to the theme park.  I took them to the park and then went home.

It’s another home alone Saturday afternoon for me.  It again became so hard.  I got so emotional and sorrowful, cried many times.  I called mom’s cell phone.  She was having rough time too.  She was crying while waiting in line for the ride.  She was crying during the ride.  And because of her terrible emotion, even the very mild ride made her very sick.  So, Priscilla has to take most of the rides by herself (Esther and Deborah went on their own with their friends to the more thrilled rides, and grandma could not do any ride.)  Looking at Priscilla got on the ride by herself made mom cry.  It would be nice that Lydia could accompany Priscilla…

 

In the evening, we had a small group evangelical meeting at a friend’s house.  Everybody there knew us.  Again, we talked about Lydia and something else.  To several unbelievers, Lydia’s death did not change them a bit.  They felt sorry for us.  And that was it.  They could not think more than that.  I guess if a person does not believe in God, then he does not have answer to any of these life and death questions.  So, the best way is not to think and continue their old way of life. 

I felt very sad and sorry for them.  I have been praying for their salvation for a long time (years) already.  May the Holy Spirit, can lead them turn to God…
