June 27, 2006, Tuesday

It’s a quiet day for Lydia (just rest at home, still not able to eat much, nutrition mostly from feeding tube), while her siblings were having tennis games, piano lessons, and swimming meets.  I do not know how she feels when she stuck at home with Mom, while everybody else in the family were out having good times.  Indeed, life goes on, although Lydia’s life now is pretty much filled with a lot of suffering.  I can only pray for the peace and joy in Lydia’s heart from our heavenly father.

 

The doctor called, he wanted us to do another blood test tomorrow morning.  If the blood counts are still low, we will need to go back to hospital for blood transfusion.  That is not fun at all.

We cast everything is God’s hand.  We pray that Lydia’s blood count would increase in tomorrow’s blood test.
