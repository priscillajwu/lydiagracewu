April 4, 2006, Tuesday

After each radiation these days, Lydia felt very down, and cried often. Her scalp hurts, stomach hurts badly, and throat hurts whenever she talks.

"Could God take away my pain?" Lydia asked, "When will my life be normal again?

"Well, you know the answers to all of that." Mom Said, "Does whining help?"

Lydia shook her head. "But I am so painful, I don’t feel good."

"So, what should we do?" Mom asked.

"Pray." Lydia answered.

"Lord Jesus, please take away my pain, please take away my pain…." Lydia prayed.

It was difficult for Mom at those moments.

Besides, Lydia did not like her look with very few hairs. She thought that it was creepy. Well, to tell the truth, it is a little scary for little kids to look at it.

"I think we should shave all the hair now." Mom told Lydia.

Lydia did not answer.

"How about if Daddy did it with you?" Mom asked again.

Lydia nodded and smiled.

So, we had a hair cut tonight. Everybody took turns to cut Daddy and Lydia’s hair. It was a lot of fun for the entire family.

Lydia does look a lot nicer now. Indeed, she looks very cute.

Praise the Lord. We should be joyful and content. (Philippians 4:12-13)

In Him

Simon
