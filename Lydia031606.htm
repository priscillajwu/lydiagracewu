3/16/06 from Jean Wu

It’s hard to believe that we were back to hospital so soon.  We went home After Lydia had recovered from her second brain surgery on 3/3. I knew we would start our daily radiation treatment hospital visit but not in-patient treatment. The shock was that I didn’t think she would have her side effect until much later. So why is she still suffering serious headache and even throwing up? After whole day at hospital, I just realized this is just beginning of “ups and downs”, and “in and out of hospital”. As Simon told me that we need to be more humble before God, things may be looking better, but we have nothing to boast about. God’s will is higher than ours!

When I’m looking at my little tiny Lydia (she is getting smaller by not eating and sleeping well), I cried sometimes. It’s so much suffering for her. It made her strong in a such tough way.

Today we got to take an Ambulance to University Hospital for her second regular radiation treatment since Lydia has not been discharged from hospital, we were not allowed to go by our own. It’s quite an experience for all of us.

Lydia is still suffering from pain and nauseous feeling. Doctors only treat her symptoms now. Hopefully we can get the symptoms under control, and plan to go home tomorrow.
