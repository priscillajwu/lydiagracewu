October 18, 2006

 

Dear Friends,

 

Lydia Grace Wu was our young 9-year-old girl who bravely faced the struggles of a malignant brain tumor for seven months before departing this world on August 21, 2006, right after she turned age 10. She inspired thousands of people with her life, and her story continues to inspire multitudes today. Her story can be shared through this band, spurring people on to truly live life with faith, hope, and love.  Encouraging everyone who hears it to truly Live It All.

 

For more information on Lydia’s story, please visit her website:

 

http://www.immanuel.net/masoncc/Lydia_Update/UpdateCalendar.htm

 

In effort to share Lydia's story and her undying faith with the world, we have come up with a plan to circulate as many Lydia wristbands as we can. In doing so keep Lydia's memory alive sharing her faith and story with all those who see the bright red wristband. (Lydia's favorite color of course!) Let this band encourage you to NEVER GIVE UP and Live It ALL.

 

If you get one this spirit filled wristband, you will help to support missionaries (which always is Lydia’s dream to be a missionary herself) and set up a Lydia’s memorial Scholarship Fund to help needed students.

 

We are asking for a dollar donation for each wristband.

Please share your love through getting this wristband.

We love you!
