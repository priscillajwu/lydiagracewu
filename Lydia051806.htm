May 18, 2006, Thursday

Thank you for your prayer, Lydia’s temperature has come down today. Praise the Lord.

Lydia visited school briefly in the morning, soon not feeling well and had to rush home.

Recall that Lydia rarely missed school since kindergarten. Her attendance at school was perfect. She very seldom got sick. Rare occasion got fever (probably less than a few times), she would recover very quickly. But now, only a mild fever, it would take several days just to get slightly better. Really, everything we have or own is from God, nothing we can boast about. He gave and He could take away.  May His name be praised.  (Job 1:21)

Lydia collected a lot of vegetables from our backyard with mom in the late afternoon. They gave away the vegetables to the neighbors. She was really excited to do that. To give is really what Lydia like very much. That makes her very happy.
