March 5, 06

My dear Prayer heroes,

Thank you for your prayers, over the weekend, Lydia was doing quite well. Being around with the sisters made her very happy. She also got to enjoy various delicious food and dishes from the love of the brothers and sisters.

For the past two nights, after came home from hospital on Friday, Jean and I had good nights of sleep. At first, we kind of expected that Lydia would wake up during the night, and we would need to help her and calm her down before medicine taking effect, just like in the hospital. On the contrary, Lydia did wake up, two or three times, during the night because of the pain. But she would take pain medicine herself, and then tried to go back to sleep without waking up Jean or me. We were so moved when we heard that. She is so considerate, strong, sweet, and pleasant. We are so thankful. Praise the Lord.

Lydia was given an award, from OMTA (Ohio Music Teachers Association) for her courageous determination, passion and commitment that, only two weeks after her first brain surgery, and knowing she needed second brain surgery two days later, she still attended the piano competition. Lydia was very happy when she saw the awards tonight, received by mom during the winner concert.

A friend came to visit Lydia today, and praised Lydia for being so brave during all of these.

"I have Lord Jesus to help me," Lydia replied, "I don’t know how can a person who does not believe in God go through this."

I felt exactly the same way. When I was in ultimate sorrow, heart ache, and desperation, it’s God who put peace into my heart in a miraculous way. He also let us know that many, so many, people are praying for Lydia, caring about her, and loving her sincerely. Lydia got a lot of comforts from non-stopping cards, emails, little gifts, and visits... These also lifted up her spirits.

God cares Lydia in great ways, and through people around us, near by or far away. This is His healing power. We have experienced it, and we believe more will come.

"Do you know why is there still residual tumor?" Jean shared with me tonight, "So we will see God’s healing power and God’s glory."

"And we will be humble before God." I added.

This was a wonderful weekend. Really, thank God and praise the Lord.

In Him,

Simon
