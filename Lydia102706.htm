October 27, 2006, Friday

It is very difficult to be left behind when Lydia departed the world.

I am thinking today, even I want to be with the Lord, which is much better, but for the sake of others, our love ones, I should live as long as possible, even outlive my wife or my children (I already did it once anyway).  This is at least I could do, for the love ones, and for the Lord.

I want to be like Lydia, with the characters from the Lord: Living it ALL, with Love, FAITH, and Hope.

 

Then, I think further, It would be a lot lot worse to be left behind after the Lord’s day, the Rapture day.  The loss, the frustration, the ultimate pain, and forever hopelessness, and meaningless living, how can that be bearable?

Shouldn’t we all come to the Lord as soon as possible? Like now?
