June 10, 2006, Saturday

I promised Lydia that before her first chemo treatment. We would take her away from the crowd and stay in the woods for a couple days. She was very looking forward to it.

Wednesday night’s emergency room stay really got us scared. But to Lydia’s mind, the most worry she had was that we would not be able to go on vacation.

Thank the Lord, for He has mercy. We did make it. We rent a cottage in the woods, and stayed there and enjoyed a retreat. Lydia had a great time.

She even tried horseback riding, first time in her life riding a horse by herself. She rode the horse for a full 30 minutes, going through the trails in the woods.

Lydia, my little girl, never stopped amazing me for many surprises.

I praise the Lord for that.

One more day of rest, she will start her chemo treatment. Mom was under quite a bit of stress. But we know everything is in God’s hand.
