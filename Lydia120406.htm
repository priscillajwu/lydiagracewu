December 4, 2006

 

sometimes i just feel like...all walls are crashing down on me. and that life is really not worth living. nothing i want works out. and then i think...
that's cuz...it's true that if life was just everything i wanted working out...it would truly not be worth living.
instead, despite all the impossibilities i feel from day to day, i know that what happens isn't what i want, but it is GOD'S will!...and i might not understand it now. i may not understand fully why my little sister had to get cancer, and why she then had to die while so many others with her same sickness lived right in front of my eyes. and leaving behind a broken family.
i truly don't understand. but i've seen little clues that God has revealed. and i know that my little sis is with Him now. and as much i wish that I was there right now...i'm not. but i can long to be home as much as i want and still be doing God's work. Lydia already did her job...so very well. and her Father's taken her home.
now it's my turn. it's my turn to live. and til the day that God decides to take me home, and God let it be soon, i plan on doing so...to the fullest
