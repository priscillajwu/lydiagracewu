June 28, 2006, Wednesday

I got up early in the morning (although stayed up extremely late last night, actually, this morning) with heavy burden in my heart, wondering why Lydia’s blood counts were still low. According to the doctors, most of the children chemo patients would have blood counts up by now…

I prayed and prayed. Knowing God has been carrying us through many difficult situations, why would this time be any different?

I deeply believe and have faith in our Lord. However, I was very anxious to find out the blood test results.

Jean called. THE NUMBERS WERE UP. Praise the Lord. I was so relieved.  There was no need of blood transfusion.

Our God again answered our prayers. What we are experiencing is that our God is faithful, He is so real, so true, so comforting, and provides us great hope deep in our hearts and souls…

We are truly blessed. Although the journey is hard, but it’s the life lesson we have to take, the lesson God specially designed for us.

Thank you for your prayers.
