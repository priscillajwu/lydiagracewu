March 24, 2005

My dear prayer heroes,

Lydia has been staying in the hospital since Monday, the same length as her second brain surgery. She is still in so much pain. She is not ready to go home yet, however, doctors think we could go home for the weekend. This is the first time Lydia has not felt comfortable returning home.

After many doctors and nurses coming to talk her, and the pain specialty doctor leaving his pager and personal cell phone number to us; finally, Lydia agreed.  We arrived home at around 5 PM.

It’s so nice to be home, Lydia’s sisters rushed to talk and play with her right away.  She was happy.  I truly believe her three sisters are the best medicine.

 She kept asking for us to bring her out to store to buy a gift for Esther’s birthday.  So we did.  Using the gift card she received, Lydia bought a gift for Esther…..

 In Him

Simon
