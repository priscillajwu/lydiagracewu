April 24, 2006, Monday

My dear prayer heroes,

In today’s clinic check up, we were able to get the ear drops medicine and ointment for Lydia’s skin itchiness. 

“Would it help a little bit?”  I asked.

“Actually, it should help a lot.”  Doctor answered.

Thank the Lord, we can have the medicines to help relieve Lydia’s discomfort.

Lydia still did not eat much.  It’s so frustrating for all of us.  We tried to feed her.  She just had hard time to swallow anything.  Often, sip of pure water was all she could do.

Mom and girls were singing a contemporary Christian song which mom liked a lot.  Mom said she would like this song in her funeral.  This made Lydia cry.  She cried loudly and said this will make her think about how mommy is taking care of her now….

Mom has noticed that Lydia could not hear the phone rings when Lydia was in certain area in the house.  She used to be very alert and always was able to hear it.  Hearing loss is one of the possible side effects or permanent damages from radiation.  We pray that this is only temporary.  We pray that nothing would happen to Lydia unless our Lord allows it, and He has perfect plan for it.

Our God has great mercy.

In Him,

Simon
