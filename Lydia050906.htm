May 9, 2006, Tuesday

We prayed hard yesterday about Lydia’s eating problem ….

Usually Lydia would try to avoid the meals. She would mention she was not feeling good, nauseous, or tired.

Tonight, the same way, we had hard time to get her on the dinner table.

However, we have food prepared by a family from the church. The dishes looked very delicious.

Normally I would do the prayer. But today, I made Lydia pray and give thanks to the Lord.

We all enjoyed the food a lot, except Lydia. She still only watched around without even giving a try. Mom tried to feed her slowly, like feeding a baby (that reminded me mother’s day is coming). Gradually, she started eating by herself, and more and more. She liked it. At the end, she ate a lot. We were so joyful. According to mom, this is the largest amount in one meal for Lydia recently.

It’s really a miracle. It’s also a great comfort from our Lord for He answered our prayers.

We also noticed today’s food is spicier. Lydia’s taste buds probably got damaged by the radiation to some extent. Spicy food may help. Thank God for showing us some hints to improve her appetite.

We pray that Lydia’s will start to eat better, gain weight, and be ready for the first chemo therapy which was scheduled to be May 30.

Like to share happy moments with you. Praise the Lord.
