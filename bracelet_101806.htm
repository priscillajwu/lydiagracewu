October 18, 2006

Lydia Grace Wu was a young 9-year-old girl who bravely faced the struggles of a malignant brain tumor for seven months before departing this world. She inspired thousands of people with her life, and her story continues to inspire multitudes today. Her story can be shared through this band, spurring people on to truly live life with faith, hope, and love.

Encouraging everyone who hears it to truly Live It All.

For more information on Lydia’s story, please visit her website: http://www.immanuel.net/masoncc/Lydia_Update/UpdateCalendar.htm

 

*Suggested donation - $1 per piece

Proceeds will go to Lydia Memorial Scholarship Fund and to help sponsor missionaries.

 

 

As of December 31, 2006, $3000 US dollars have been donated to Thailand Children Mission Fund of Cincinnati Chinese Church

February 2007, Lydia Grace Wu Memorial Scholarship has been set up in Sycamore High School,

“This scholarship is awarded to a senior who exemplifies the qualities of Lydia Grace Wu by demonstrating loving, caring, always trying her best, never giving up and has hope in spite of overwhelming odds.”
