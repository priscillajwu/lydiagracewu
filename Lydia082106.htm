August 21, 2006, Monday

Last night, mom slept with Lydia on the same bed, since then, Lydia has been breathing fine. However, since this morning, her oxygen level has been low for a few times. The last time, we could not get her back.

Lydia passed away in her sleep around 3:30 PM.

The pain and sorrow is beyond I could describe.

We thank the Lord for taking Lydia’s soul. She is now resting in the Lord. No more suffering. Lydia knew that is the best place.

We miss her so much. We will meet her again in the Lord.

Thank God for the hope and promise.

If you have any thoughts or memories about Lydia and her story, please share with us through email RememberingLydia@zoomtown.com
