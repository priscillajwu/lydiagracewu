March 6, 06

My dear prayer heroes,

 

Today, one week after Lydia’s second brain surgery, she was able to go to Barnes and Noble and Family Christian Bookstore with Mom.  Lydia was going to buy a few books she longed for some time, and using the gift certificate she received recently.

After around 30 minutes, Lydia felt tired and not feeling too well.  Before they came home, they noticed that, in Christian bookstroe, they could adopt (buy) a stuffed animal (lamb), which will be given to an orphan, who are being treated in Children hospital.  Lydia really wanted to do that.

“Can I ?” Lydia asked mom.

“Of course.” Mom answered.  Lydia gets to choose how to spend the money from the love of many uncles and aunts.

So, she did.  She bought a stuffed lamb animal, which will be given to a sick child.  The love of our Lord have been flowing from you, to Lydia, and to another child…..

I recall the first day Lydia was admitted into ICU.  She lay on the regular adult size bed.  A small body on a big cold hospital bed, not only a bit awkward, it was very sad to see that.  That night, sisters brought Lydia’s favorite stuff animal to her.  That stuff animal lay on her bed side since then, till now.  It did help.  We pray that this lamb will bring some comfort to the child who will be getting it.

Lydia bought a few books, she enjoyed reading them after coming home.  Thank you and praise the Lord.

 

In Him,

Simon
