December 7, 2006, Thursday

A dear brother in Christ sent email to us today,

“I was very touched by today's sharing.  It is so neat that Esther's friend was changed; it gives us a glimpse of the impact of Lydia's situation.  We will never know the full impact of her life on this world till we get to heaven but it is a grace of God to show us some of HIS plan.

 

Also encouraged to see Jean be used to comfort others despite her sorrow.  The few months you have had since Lydia went home is not enough for your heart and soul to grieve yet to see you and Jean minister in the midst of your pain is a testimony to your faith and God is already using you to help others who are not so strong.  Many will be tempted to leave the faith when life is hard and seeing your example will help those understand that life is about God and not ourselves and HE is bigger than our hurts and pains and someday we will experience joy and happiness forever.  But we must persevere.”

Yes, we must persevere.  Thank you, brother.

 

Today, mom went to middle school, where Lydia supposed attending if she were still alive.  Mom was presented with many books donated to the school from friends, students and parents in memory of Lydia.  It was so nice.  We were very touched.

Thank you, thank you very much.

 

I do not know why Lydia had to die.  But one thing I am sure is that God has been here with us all the time, and He will be watching over us always.
