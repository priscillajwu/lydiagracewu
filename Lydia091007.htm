Living It All

By: Angela Lee

 

Live life to the fullest
Live it all

For you may never know

When it will slip away from your hands

Live life to the fullest
Live it all

Get the best out of it

Like making lemonade out of lemons

Live life to the fullest
Live it all

Down the narrow road

Hang on tightly to your friends

Help you in your fight

Live life to the fullest
Live it all

Give encouragement

Be caring

Show love and

Spread hope

Live life to the fullest
Live it all

With a smile on your face

You can face the day ahead of you

Because God will lead the way

He’ll hold you

And guide you

Through the hardest of times

You can always trust Him in the middle of the night

Show faith in times of despair

Stick together and be there for each other

That way

You’ll make it through

Even if God takes you home

In the middle of it all

But still

Live it all

 

*Dedicated to Lydia Grace Wu©
