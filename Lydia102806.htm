October 28, 2006, Saturday

I had very bad stomachache today (even at this moment, still painful).  So my patience and temper were not very good, even during the church meeting in the evening.

Then, of course, I thought about Lydia.  She was under so many different kinds of sufferings at the same time, terrible stomachache was only one of them.  However, she was able to take and endure it without much complaint.

I felt extremely ashamed.  Then, I prayed for the strength from the Lord.  It helped.  The ache was still there, but I was able to take it better.

Lydia was a living miracle from the Lord.  I witnessed her being so strong.  A nine year-old girl was so amazing.  That has to be the grace from the Lord.
