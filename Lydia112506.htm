November 25, 2006, Saturday

We had another gathering with several families which we have known one another even before Esther was born.

We shared and counted God’s blessings: our children, their education, being healthy, financial planning, etc...

What in my mind was that all of these were low level blessings.  From the book I am reading now, the author suggested that, sometimes, God would take away the low level blessings in order for us to experience the high level blessing which is God Himself.  So we could experience God deep in our soul.

Unfortunately, with low level blessings being taken away, many people became bitter or discouraged, or even against the Lord, before getting the true high level blessing.

It is truly painful when the low level blessings were taken away from us.  It’s happening to us now.  I should not be surprised. (1 Peter 4:12)  I have a lot more to learn in life.
