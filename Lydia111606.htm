Thursday, November 16, 2006

 

Lydia liked to read.

One of her favorite series books is Unfortunate Events by Lemony Snicket. She read every single one except the 13th book in the series is the last one called The End. She has been waiting for that book to come out. But she passed away before the book came out. She just missed it!

 

I bought the book of “The End” at Priscilla’s school Book Fair several days ago.

 

“Lydia doesn’t get the chance to read her last one of favorite book.” I said sadly when I brought the book home to show to Priscilla.

 

“This is her favorite book, Lydia must already read it in heaven. She must even read it before we get the book.” Priscilla said it firmly.

 

Priscilla added, “Heaven is a place called paradise, Lydia would get what she likes. Don’t worry about it!”

 

Heaven sounds so good in my girls’ views!!

 

 

Priscilla never read this series of books before. She starts to read after I bought the last one of this series. She likes them, but we don’t have the 2nd book. She begged me to buy it in order to complete the series.

 

Today I went to Barnes & Noble to get the 2nd book of the Unfortunate Events using the gift card Lydia received during her illness. I found out this is the last gift card, too. Everything will eventually come to the end. Sooner or later, no matter you like it or not.  Time has no sympathy.

 

Life goes on! The fact that Lydia is gone would never change! The earth still keeps turning. Everyone has life to move on, even myself have to be forced to move on without any sympathy. I have no control for time keeps passing by.

 

I have to reevaluate my life’s purpose for both of the long term and short term goals.

Live for Christ!

But how? And what?

 

Should we order more wristband?  A few thousands of them were almost gone.

We would like to publish Lydia’s story.  We need a publisher.

 

Please pray for us.
