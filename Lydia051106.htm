May 11, 2006, Thursday

My dear prayer heroes,

Praise the Lord, Lydia’s white blood cell (WBC) count has increased to 2.3 units from 1.9 one week ago (1.5 two weeks ago). The normal range is 4.5 to 13.5 units. She has two and half weeks more to go before the first chemo therapy.  Although rebound speed is slow, we have faith that Lydia will be OK because our God is amazing and faithful.

Lydia spent a few hours attending classes at school today. She tried her best to participate all the class activities. A girl from her class said, "…Lydia doesn’t look like she has brain tumor…"

What a wonderful comment! Praise the Lord. Thank God for the encouragement like this, from a little girl.

Lydia threw up today, (so much amount, mom was astonished), and it’s also the first time for more than two weeks. After that, she would not eat anything. Looked at her pale face, very little we could do, just pray…

Lydia is going to attend "Relay for Life" as one of the "cancer survivors", the event sponsored by American Cancer Society tomorrow evening. We pray that Lydia has strength and good spirits.

In Him,

Simon
