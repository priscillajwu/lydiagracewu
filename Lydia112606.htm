November 26, 2006, Sunday

After several days of feast, mom and girls gained some weight noticeably.  As of me, my weight did not change at all.

Lydia and I were two family members who have the good metabolism.  We both did not gain weight easily, no matter how much we eat.  Mom was often jealous about that. 

But, is having a high metabolism a good thing?  I am not sure.

Lydia had hard time to gain weight, and she lost weight easily.  During her illness, losing so much weight definitely did not help.  She became so skinny.  I got very stressed that she could not eat, could not gain weight.

Most of people worried about over-weight.  Oddly, the thought hurts me.  I simply wish Lydia could be somewhat heavier.

 

We rent a DVD movie from the video store.  It’s a comedy.  It was quite funny.  Daddy and three girls had a lot of laughs while watching the movie.  Later, I realized that Mom and Lydia watched this movie in one of the hospital stays.

“Did Lydia like it?  Did she enjoy it?”  I asked mom anxiously.

“Not really.  She just watched, and did not show much interest in it.”  Mom replied sadly.

Truly, when pain and suffering overwhelm, life is just plainly difficult.

 

Late in the night, all girls went to bed already.  Mom and dad, for whatever reason, became extremely sad and in terrible sorrow.  We cried and cried and cried.  It was miserable.  It’s so hard to bear…
