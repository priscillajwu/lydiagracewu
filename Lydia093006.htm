September 30, 2006, Saturday

Twice in a row, last night and this morning, when we shared gospel to people, we got response something like,

“We are doing pretty good.  We don’t need your God.  Besides, from what happen to you, I do not see the blessing from your God.”

Wow, that hurts.  I know they did not do that on purpose.  They only said that because I would like to share the good news to them.  But people will take whatever excuses to reject God.

When we told them that, without God, we would not be able to go on with situation like what we are now.  They would respond that they don’t think this kind of unfortunate events would happen to them.  If it happened, then they will deal with it.  Why bother now.

Typical secular atheist response.  Seek for happiness, deal with the problem when it comes.  You Christian think too much.  I have too many things to worry about.  I am too busy.  I don’t have time.

 

Only several hours later, God showed His grace and power.

There was an evangelical meeting tonight, mainly aimed for the ethnical Chinese from Cincinnati and nearby cities.  We are the host church.  We have been praying for this meeting for months.  I was not able to get involved much due to Lydia’s illness early on.  The preparation of this meeting has been going in parallel with Lydia’s condition got worse.  Now Lydia has passed away, but the work of God’s kingdom continues.

Over one thousand people attended.  We had great speakers and music.  At the end of meeting, dozens of people are willing to follow Jesus.  Quite a few of them from Cincinnati area have been following Lydia’s story.  I looked at them, and was very moved by the grace of God.

Each soul is so precious.  Earthly life will end one day, just matter of time.  Where to spend the eternity is essential.

Lydia’s story definitely made people think…
