November 3, 2006, Friday

Mom was running around entire day to many places and schools today.

Quite a few people, in various situations, shared with mom about how their lives were changed, how their faith with the Lord got a lot deeper, because of Lydia’s story. 

Mom heard it from the high school Esther attending, from junior high school Deborah attending, and the orthodontist office.  Many amazing stories.

In the evening during bible study, I heard that the accelerated math teacher in middle school (which Lydia supposed to attend if she were still alive), had the class picture.  Somehow, she put Lydia’s picture in it with the entire class.  That warmed our hearts so much.

At night, we received an email from a high school sophomore from another state,

 

“……

I have learned to appreciate life, and i have recently accepted Jesus in my heart and have been baptized. Lydia's story gave me the courage and showed me that life is so short and there is no time to waste. I hope that you know how many people you have touched and inspired. My life has taken a whole different turn, and i just wanted to thank you. Your daughter has been a major part of me turning my life around. 

……”

 

Thank the Lord for He comforted us greatly with many stories from the Kingdom of God.

Praise the Lord.
