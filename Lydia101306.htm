October 13, 2006, Friday

Friday night at church, many children, including several Lydia’s good friends are having a lot of fun memorizing bible verses and playing games.  It’s Friday night routine.

To be able to have regular routine is quite a blessing already.

I recall many times, Lydia cried and said,

“I just want to be a regular kid.”

And she did try very hard, although not feeling well, under extreme discomfort and sick, nauseous, she tried to keep her routine as much as possible.  It’s heart breaking just think of that.

Doing routine, many would think it’s boring.  To some, like Lydia, it’s luxury.

God did not give Lydia a regular kid’s life.  It was short, very short.  But her spirit and her life story has deeply impacted me, her father forever, as well as many others.

Praise the Lord.
