February 14, 2008, Thursday

The Valentine’s Day.

Another difficult day.

I rather treat it as a regular day.

However, I managed to go to the store and get presents for mom and three girls (although I wish I could get four presents for four girls like before).  Then, I feel better (not as guilty) that I still need to grieve badly at this Valentine’s Day.

 

God gave me some light today, about many great servants of the Lord.  What they said to God when they were in the low point in their life.

Moses, “if this is how you are going to treat me, put me to death right now”, Numbers 11:15

Elijah, “I have had enough, Lord.  Take my life; I am no better than my ancestors.” 1 Kings 19:4

Job, “Why did I not perish at birth, and die as I came from the womb?” Job 3:11

Jonah, “Now, O Lord, take away my life, for it is better for me to die than to live.” Jonah 4:3.

 

Of course, God did not grant them wish, because God has a much better plan in their life.

 

Most importantly, the Lord gave me the precious teachings from apostle Paul, “I desire to depart and be with Christ, which is better by far; but it is more necessary for you that I remain in the body.  Convinced of this, I know that I will remain, and I will continue with all of you for your progress and joy in the faith.”  Philippians 1:23-25.

 

At this Valentine’s Day, nothing much happened, but the Lord has again convinced me that there will be trouble and sufferings in this world, but the Lord has overcome the world.  John 16:33

We just need to persevere to the end.  Hebrews 10:36
