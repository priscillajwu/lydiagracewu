November 13, 2006, Monday

We received a video store gift card, which was intended to give to Lydia for her birthday presents.  However, knowing Lydia was in critical condition, they explained, that they planned to give to us after Lydia got better.  Of course, Lydia never got better since then.  They decided to give us the gift card now.  We shed tears while reading their letter.  Being also brothers and sisters in Christ, they mentioned they would explain to Lydia when they meet in heaven why the present was late…

How nice, the hope and promise we all have, to be reunited in the Lord at the end.

 

Grandma called today and asked me if I were too busy. (For decades, she always worried that I got too busy and did not take care of myself.)  I stuck on the phone and didn’t know how to answer her.

Really, it is very strange that I lost the sense of feeling busy or not.  I do not know if I am busy or I am not busy.  The world seems lost the control over me.  I am sure my life still goes on.  Everyday is filled with a lot of stuff, work, church meetings, and driving around with three girls.  However, busy or not is not something I would care or think about.  I tried very hard to live everyday (although I still consider I am emotionally weak and handicapped).  But I tried very hard.

I would never forget that life is very short, I ought to live everyday like this is the last day of my life.  Busy or not, is it really matter?  All I need to make sure is that I lived for God today.
