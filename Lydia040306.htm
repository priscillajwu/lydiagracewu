April 3, 2006 Monday

My dear prayer Heroes,

Lydia lost even more hair today.

Tonight, we heard a screaming from bathroom, and a loud crying.  It was Lydia. She was scared by her own look. 

“It’s scary.” She said and cried badly. 

Mom held her up, and crying too.

I was deeply saddened by the situation.  I have to try very hard to hold my tears.  I told Lydia, “It’s not scary, you’re just not used to it yet.  Look at mirror a few more times, you will get used to it.”

“No, I don’t want to.  It’s scary.”  Lydia has been avoiding look into the mirror since then.

It’s very difficult for all of us.  We pray that her hair can fall as soon as possible or she is willing to cut it.  It may look nicer.

Her radiation has causing her a lot of pains all over her body, as well as weakness.  According to the doctor, the situation may not get better until first part of radiation finishes, which is another week and half.  Then, second part of radiation will start the day after.  We have only two and half weeks into the radiation, three and half more weeks ahead of us.

Her condition is already not in good shape.  We pray that she will start to get better tomorrow, no matter what doctor said.  We pray in despair….

Our heavenly father has mercy and grace.  Please pray for Lydia.

Thank you very much and God bless.

In Him,

Simon
