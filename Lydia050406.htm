May 4, 2006, Thursday

Lydia did not go to school today, since she had doctor appointments.

Thank you for your prayers, Lydia’s blood count has gone up some. We (and doctors) were very happy. Although the numbers were still much lower than normal range, Lydia should not have problem to perform the school musical this weekend.

Mom has been helping Lydia to take bath or shower these days. But tonight, mom was really too busy to help. She told Lydia to take shower by herself. (Lydia should be able to do that, just did not have enough confidence yet.)

Lydia cried and very hesitated, but still she took her clothes and walked into the bathroom with tears on her face.

Deborah saw it and followed Lydia into bathroom and asked, "Do you need help?"

"Yeah, can you wait here while I take shower, just in case I do not have the strength to stand."

So, Deborah stood there in the bathroom, waiting for Lydia to take shower. Just in case Lydia may need help. Wasn’t it sweet?

Priscilla has a little bit of cough today. Tonight, Lydia and Deborah were watching the school year book together. Priscilla came over sitting next to Lydia and coughed. Lydia immediately jumped up and got away from Priscilla. Priscilla got so upset, she did not know why Lydia was doing that. Lydia said, "I am afraid to get sick."

"Why don’t you say so." Priscilla said, "I felt so bad when you did that." Well, life is difficult for us sometimes.

Usually, Priscilla shared the bedroom with Lydia. But, tonight, Priscilla has to sleep in the guess room alone…

There are ups and downs. But we have hope in our Lord. Situations have gone better. And I have faith the trend will continue.
