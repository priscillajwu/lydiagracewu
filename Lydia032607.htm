March 26, 2007, Monday

It’s time to update the church directory.  Yesterday, at church, after Sunday sermon, every family got a form to fill up the directory information.

The situation like this was very difficult.  I felt sad.  As I filled in our address, and the names of myself, mom, Esther, Deborah, and then…, I put down Priscilla as the next one…

At that time, I showed my form to the sister sat next to me (luckily mom was teaching Sunday school yesterday, she escaped another heart breaking situation).  I expressed to her I was very reluctant to fill out the form.

“You should put Lydia’s name there.”  She suggested, “Except she lives in heaven now.)

“Yeah, it’s a good idea.”  I replied.

Although Lydia does not live in the address on earth, she is forever part of the family.  She is with the Lord now.  Her address is now in heaven.

I happily put down Lydia’s name in the form.  Felt much better.

 

How desperate it would be for people who do not believe in God if they experience the death of the loved one.  The devil may have so many ways, explanations, or theories to convince (deceive) them or get them go on for their lives.  But that is so sad and cruel.

 

I pray that God’s grace and salvation go to everyone (and their families and friends) who knows about Lydia’s story.

 

 

Finally, mom took the courage to put away Christmas tree.  Yes, it has been there all along since late last year.  Nobody could manage to touch it.  To put those ornaments into box?  There were quite a few ornaments made by Lydia.  Deborah and Priscilla helped a little bit.  But not for long.  It’s just too much, too heavy at heart for them to work on this project.

“It was so difficult.”  Mom called me after she finished, “It was just too difficult…”
