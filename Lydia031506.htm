My dear prayer heroes,

Lydia had her first radiation treatment today.  She was not supposed to feel anything.  She came home and felt all right.

A few hours after Lydia came home.  She started having serious headache, and then threw up.

Around evening, we were back to Children hospital emergency room.  The initial CT scan showed something swollen in her head.  Need to do MRI tomorrow.

I had difficult moments today.  Prayed a lot.  God have given me peace and let me know things will be OK.  Lydia is in God’s hand.  We have peace for He so loves Lydia.  He had delivered us from big peril, and He will deliver us again.

Please continue praying for Lydia.  We’ll have busy schedule tomorrow in the hospital. We need to find out why her head continue having pain. 

God gave me comfort tonight with the hymn “It is Well with My Soul”.  It has given me strength, not sorrow, and definitely no tear either.

In Him
