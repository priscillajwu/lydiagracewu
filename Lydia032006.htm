March 20, 06

Lydia had severely headache and nausea this morning.  After radiation therapy, she was admitted into hospital again around noon.  The doctors are trying to get an MRI either later today or tomorrow morning.

 

Lydia said to Mom, “I have so much suffering, I’d rather to be in the heaven with the Lord.”

This made Mom very sad.  When I heard this from Mom.  I was saddened as well.  It’s too much suffering for little Lydia.  She understood a lot and thought a lot too.

But, really, we are also so blessed, we have the ultimate hope to be with the Lord.  Many would not be able to say that, either they don’t believe there is a heaven, or they are not sure.  Truly, we are blessed that we can say, we will be with the Lord eventually, that is the ultimate peace and comfort.

No earthly suffering can be worse than the separation from our creator forever.
