January 27, 2007, Saturday

 

It’s about the time when Lydia started her headache one year ago.  Thinking about her suffering made me heartache terribly.  She continued severe suffering from then until she died.  Bad MRI results came one after another.  It’s so unbearable just to think of it.  There were so much suffering, for such a young age, yet she had little complaining, but showed great faith and hope.  She was so brave looking at the huge radiation machine, and asked a lot of questions.  She could pronounce and spell every chemo medicine she was receiving.  She wanted to get well...

It made me want to scream for Lydia, “IT’S SO UNFAIR”.

No hope from the current world.  I only hope that our God will reward her in heaven for she has to suffer on earth.  And that is our comfort.  Nowhere we can get from the earth.

 

Tonight, we had an open house for bible study cell group meeting.  It’s the very first time since one year ago that we had open house.  We had a Chinese Lunar new year special gathering at my house exactly one year ago and a lot of people came.  That was when Lydia started her mild headache, but not serious yet.  (But she had a lot of fun that night.)  Then, her headache grew worse and worse very quickly.  One week after that, she was diagnosed with brain tumor, stage four cancer.  The following year, we could not open our house because of her low blood counts and weak immune system from cancer treatments.  Then, after she passed away, the house was in chaos…

Just think of all of these gave me a lot of dread of tonight’s meeting.  However, God gave me strength.  It went well.  The meeting was great, we all learned great bible lessons and enjoyed the fellowship.

 

God is good.
