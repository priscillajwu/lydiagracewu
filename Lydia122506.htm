December 25, Monday, 2006

It’s the Christmas day.

Being so sad, I could not fall asleep until 4 AM on the Christmas eve (actually this morning) and read the book about grieving.  Well, I didn’t expect I would have easy time to get through the Christmas anyway.

This morning, three girls open their presents and they were all happy.  Wow, thank God for that.  Everything went fine as smooth as a family with THREE kids.

Then, we went to a gathering with several Christian families to celebrate Christmas.  It was good.  I played table tennis for a while, one of my favorite sports and I haven’t done it for years.

Then, we went to another dear sister in Christ to spend a few hours with her.  It was very good too.  Good sharing and the girls even made a pretty wreath.

In the evening, a dear family friend, a young sister in the Lord, who shares the same first name and last name with Lydia came over to spend the night with the girls.  All the girls were happy, mom was happy, and I thank God for His grace and for all your prayers.
