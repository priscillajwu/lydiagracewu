August 21, 2008, Thursday

Two years anniversary.

Not quite sure how I got through today.  I tried to keep my mind as blank as possible.

We have received many flowers, cards, and emails today.  Thank you all very very much.

Looking at the flowers, reading the cards and emails made us cry.  But knowing there are still so many people remembering Lydia at this particular day.  It’s a lot of comfort.  We treasure your love so much.  You have warmed up our hearts.

Thank God for having you around us.

 

And life goes on, no matter how hard it has been, or will be. 

We move on, until the day we meet the Lord and reunite with Lydia.

May the name of the Lord be praised.
