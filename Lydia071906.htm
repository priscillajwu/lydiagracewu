July 19, 2006, Wednesday

Lydia’s headache was very severe this morning.  So severe that Lydia wondered anything could be done.

“MRI is already pull three days in advance, and it’s only two days away.”  Mom told Lydia, “ I don’t think doctor can do anything before they have MRI results.”

“Or can we have CT scan first?”  Lydia asked.  She must be very concerned herself too.  Her pain was so great, she did not know what to do.

Mom called me.  We had a lot of prayers.  I didn’t know anything we could do other than continue trying the medications, the nauseous medicines, the pain medicines.  And also, trying to distract her by doing some activities, mostly watch TV.

Thank God, later in the day, she was better and able to play the instruments.  It seemed that God has answered her prayer to give her a bit more strength.  To get her feel better, we even let her visited the tennis court during the evening, and tried swing a few balls.
