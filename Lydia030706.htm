March 7, 06

My dear prayer heroes,

Please pray for Lydia. She will have CT scan tomorrow (3/8) at University hospital. That is for the preparation of radiation therapy.

On Thurday, after clinic appointment with oncologist, Lydia will undergo two operations. The first is to puncture her spinal cord to draw spinal fluid for the lab to make sure there is no spread to the spinal. The second is a surgery to insert a port in her upper left chest. The port will connect to the vein next to the heart, and it's for the purpose of chemotherapy.

We were told that several of Lydia's 4th grade friends would get together during lunch time at school. They would hold hands and pray for Lydia. It must have been a beautiful scene. Praise the Lord.

Lydia's school teacher, a very nice Christian sister, visited her today. They talked for almost two hours, a lot about school. I am sure Lydia missed school very much...

When sisters came home from school, or music lessons, or sports lessons. Lydia would listen to what happened and laugh with them. I felt very sad for her. She has lost just about everything, not able to go to school or any other activities, only pain and suffering, and countless hospital visits. But she seems to be all right. All I can say is praise the Lord. It has to be the comforts from our Lord, and prayers of many.....

In Him,

Simon
