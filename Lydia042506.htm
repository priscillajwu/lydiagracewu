April 25, 2006, Tuesday

My dear prayer heroes,

This is the 30th day of radiation treatment, only one more to go.  Two of the radiation technicians came to say good bye to Lydia and wish her well since they are off tomorrow.  They liked Lydia very much

I went to Greene middle school (Lydia will be attending this coming fall) for parent orientation.  The school staffs showed a lot of exciting activities.  The thought that Lydia may not be able to attend school because of chemo therapy treatment made me feel out of place, and a bit sad…  Well, it’s in God’s hand.  He has the best arrangement for Lydia.  We need to totally trust in Him.

Lydia was able to eat and drink, and hear better today.  It was truly wonderful.  Thank you for your prayers.  In the day like this, we want to shout out loud to thank the Lord and praise the Lord.

In Him,

Simon
