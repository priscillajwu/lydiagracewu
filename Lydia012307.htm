January 23, 2007, Tuesday

I miss Lydia so much.  My love for Lydia is so deep and my grieving has been so unbearable.  Tears have been shed too much.  My heart and gut are hurting constantly.

I realize that my heart and Lydia’s heart were closely attached.

When Lydia’s heart was ripped apart from mine and no longer reachable (August 21, 2006 afternoon, the horrible time), I was deeply wounded.  The human heart is very delicate.  The wound was so huge that I ache beyond I could describe.

Many people said my wound would eventually heal.  I am not too sure.  Even it does, the scar will be so huge that does it make any difference that it heals or not.

 

But,

One thing I believe deeply is that Lydia’s heart was also terribly wounded when ripped apart from the family.  The difference is that her heart has been healed by Christ Jesus.  With His power and tremendous grace, Lydia’s heart is without any scar.

And that is so comforting.

I thank God, and still with a lot a lot of tears…
