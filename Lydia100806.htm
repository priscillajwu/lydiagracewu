October 8, 2006, Sunday

After many days of poor quality of sleep, I took a long nap this afternoon.  It was not a peaceful one.  I woke up with deeply saddened heart.

I thought about Lydia that she tried to get up by herself in August 12 (while we all thought she was asleep and mom stepped away for about two minutes, none of us were in the room with her).  She got up by herself and fell to the floor.  She wanted to do it by herself to get up.  She wanted to get well.  She loved the family.  She wanted to go home.  She wanted to have normal life like other kids….

I was about to cry.  But Esther and Priscilla were there with me.  I have to hold.  Wow, that was not comfortable.  Later, I went to Lydia’s grave by myself and cried there as much as I could. 

 

“You love Lydia so much, that’s why you are hurting so much.”  A sister in the Lord shared with us today.

She is right.

We also love the Lord so much.  That is why we got hurt so much that He allowed this to happen.

 

But, even more so, we have to believe in God and His sovereignty.  Otherwise, we will be nowhere.

 

And I believe God does not make mistake.

I continue begging for the Lord’s comfort and mercy.
