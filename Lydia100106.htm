October 1, 2006, Sunday

I could not sleep well last night.  I felt like I was constantly being awakened and fell asleep again.  This happened several times.  I prayed and prayed when I was awake or half awake.  Really, there was nothing troubled me.  I guess it’s just the sadness lingering in my mind.

Woke up this morning, I had a very heavy heart and last for the entire day.  Irritable and emotional.  I got upset with myself a few times, and wept a lot more times.

Thank God it’s Sunday, fellowship with brothers and sisters helped.  Teaching Sunday school also gave me a great pleasure to serve the Lord.

 

Mom said to me about her thoughts,

“If Lydia had choice, this was what she wanted, she would rather go with the Lord.”  Mom said, “This is how we raised our children, and it is also our goal of life, to be with the Lord because that is much better.”

“Yes, I agree.  But did Lydia think that way before she got ill.”  I asked mom.

“Yes, she would.  As of now, all our three girls are thinking that way too.”  Mom said, “And Lydia has fulfilled the purpose of life.”

 

We all agree that Lydia has done a great job.  She fulfilled her life in just ten years.  She has gone to the better place, where we will be going too.  She is ahead of us.  However, the emotion, our love to Lydia, the memories of past ten years just again and again broke my heart.

 

Losing the child is so hard.  I can not think of anything else worse in life…

Well, one thing is worse, that is, without salvation.  Lived and died without the Lord, without the true purpose of life.  That got to be a lot lot worse…
