December 1, 2006, Friday

Received from Lydia’s very good friend’s father yesterday

“

Just wanted to let you know that I read your posts everyday and that you are constantly in my prayers.  I can't imagine the grief you are going through, but I do know that I still grieve with you.  I have learned through you, to be grateful and thank the Lord for the time that I have with my family.  While I only knew Lydia for a couple of years, I feel blessed to know her and your family.  You are truly special people and I am so impressed that you are taking the message of Lydia's life and spreading the good news of our eternal life with God.  My relationship with the Lord has been strengthened through the witness of a little 10 year old girl.  While it seems like such a long time until you reunite with Lydia, it is a very short time in the eyes of God.  It's so hard for us to know just how great our eternal life with God will be.  I don't think that we are humanly capable of understanding his greatness and why things happen like children dying.  The one thing I do know, is that Lydia fully understands what we cannot completely and is rejoicing in his presence already.  One day soon, we will all know the joy that Lydia is experiencing now and we will be reunited with all of the people we love in heaven.  
In God's Love,

“

I was able to meet him and his wife tonight at church.  We talked and shed some more tears.  Although the pain is unbearable, the Lord comforted us through many brothers and sisters around us.
