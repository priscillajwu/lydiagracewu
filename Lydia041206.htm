We have hope in our Lord, and He is faithful.

Although still not able to get hold of the doctors yet, we were able to see the preliminary radiology report of yesterday’s MRI. The MRI showed no new abnormality identified. One of the tumors has decreased the size from 12 mm to 7 mm.

We would like to share the good news with you. May all the glory go to our God. Praise the Lord.
