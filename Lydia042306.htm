April 23, 2006, Sunday

Lydia went to Sunday school this morning for the first time after her illness.  She was with her class for the whole time without Mom on the side.  It was very nice for her to come back to worship God.  She wants to get back to normal.  It was her very sincere wish nowadays.

I recall two days ago, on Friday night.  It was also her first time to come back to Awana children program.  She was able to try a few games.  She used to be able to run very fast.  However, she was falling again and again.  That did not discourage her, and she continued to try.  The falling was probably from her weakness, not something caused by her brain tumors.  The entire crowd of children was cheering for her when she ran.  That was quite an emotional moment.

We also went fishing in the evening.  For some reason, it was in Lydia’s mind for several days already.  Although with very tight schedule, we still made it.  We caught a few fish (small Blue gill) and snails, it was a lot of fun.

She had a lot of skin reaction from radiation.  It was very itchy.  She would scratch and made the situation worse.  It was very uncomfortable.  She was not able to sleep through the night.

She also had similar to ear infection type of side effect (from radiation).  That made the sleep a real challenge past few days.

Thank you for your prayers.
