January 30, 2007, Tuesday

 

One of the girls got extremely stressed, from school, from herself, from many commitments she has, and worst of all, from her bereaved parents, who are grieving terribly.

And she also has to deal with the death of the sister.

 

I got a bit scared and worried about her.  I retrieved and prayed…

 

Life is unfair.  Truly unfair.  But God will make it fair at the end, not on earth, but in eternal perspectives.

 

Life is so difficult.  Always difficult.  But with God, we can get through it.  Thank God for holding us together nicely.

 

As always, may God’s name be glorified.
