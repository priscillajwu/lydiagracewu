July 29, 2007, Sunday

The summer swimming season ended this weekend with the championship at Miami University.

Life goes on.  So I had to bring girls to participate.

It was not the easiest place for me to be, since I got emotional while recalling Lydia’s courageous swim last year, at the same place, only a few weeks before she passed away.  And at that time, cancer was probably spread already, we just did not know yet…

The girls did well.  I told them Lydia would be proud of them too.

 

Even facing the worst adversity in life, we should still live it to the fullest.  This was what Lydia showed to the world in her short life.

To remember Lydia, we are in the process of setting up the Lydia Grace Wu Memorial Fund through the Greater Cincinnati Foundation, a local, well known and established philanthropy organization.  Lydia’s memorial fund will continue to award, encourage, and provide scholarships to the students who demonstrated the same spirits of Lydia’s and missionaries (Lydia’s dream) year after year, even after my own (and mom’s) time on earth.
