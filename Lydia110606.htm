November 6, 2006, Monday

All three girls continue doing just fine.  They are able to focus on school and do what they suppose to do.  That is amazing. 

They still miss Lydia a lot though.  However, with their strong PURE faith, they truly and deeply believe Lydia is in a much nicer place now.

 

However, mom and dad continue grieving terribly.  Why is that?  Mom believed that was because we adults do not have the faith like children.  Mom observed that the same faith Lydia has, is also with three girls.

 

Mom’s eyes continue hurting, and swollen.  She is still not quite able to wear contacts.  Her eyes blurred often.  Not sure it’s tears, not used to the glasses, or the eye disease.

One thing I am sure is that she cried way too much.  But can she cry less?  I don’t think that is an easy thing to do either.
