September 24, 2006, Sunday

Deborah was baptized today.  Deborah is about the same age when Esther was baptized.  We were all extremely excited for Deborah.  All three girls hugged together afterwards, really happy. 

In a big event like this, usually, four girls would laugh, hug, hold each other, and talk a lot.  Too bad that Lydia did not and could not make it this time. 

Mom recalled in August, Lydia and mom talked about this date.  Lydia would like to get well soon to attend Deborah’s baptism.  Yes, back then, we were afraid Lydia may not be able to make it, but we never lost hope…

Tell the truth, although it’s a happy day, I was not used to the scenario that Lydia was not part of the celebration.  So, I guess I did not laugh or smile as much as I should. 

However, many friends, uncles and aunts, came to Deborah’s baptism.  People continue comforting me when we met.  It feels great surrounding with brothers and sisters in the Lord.

At night, we had a dinner banquet to celebrate Deborah’s new birth, (and grandma’s birthday happened to be around).  Again, many people came.  We had a great time together.  I shared after the dinner that, one day, a nicer banquet will be held in heaven, Lydia will join us then.  Hope every one of us will all be there.

Yes, I pray that several unbelievers in tonight’s banquet will be saved one day and become the children of God.

To lead people to the Lord, is the mission of our lives, especially Lydia’s… 

Her final wishes…

Be a missionary.

Be a person to help people to see the truth.
