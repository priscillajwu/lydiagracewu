August 2, 2007, Thursday

A couple of months ago, mom and the girls started to think about cutting their hair and donated to the Locks of Love.  I told them to wait.  So they had to endure the hot weather for the good portion of the summer, even during our vacation in Hawaii and Taiwan.  It was very hot and uncomfortable for them and a lot of effort to take care.

I was hoping as Lydia’s anniversary coming, we could do a few things, like donating the hair, setting up the memorial fund, and preparing for the memorial service, etc.  I hope these will help us ease (I really wish) our grieving and sorrow as the month of August approaches.

Also, there was something about the hair.  When Lydia applied for a wig through Locks of Love, the pure black hair was very scarce.  We could not get the hair color we would like.  Now, with four long locks of black hair, hopefully, the love can be carried on to the cancer patients who received them…

I am sure Lydia would be happy.

 

I also started working on the video clips I took during Lydia’s illness.  It has been more than one year since I took them.  It was very very very difficult, extremely painful.  I felt like I am torturing myself.  While doing it, I don’t feel like eating and my stomach really got upset, only couple days into the work, I already lost couple pounds.

Please pray for me having enough strength and help from the Lord.
