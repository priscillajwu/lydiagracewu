November 2, 2006, Thursday

For many days, mom would wake up around 4 AM (she never went to bed before mid night), and could not sleep anymore.  She would grieve and weep and wait for the dawn.  Most of time, I would be awakened as well, with very sorrowful heart, and too sad to say anything to her.

The situation like this is truly difficult.  The trial is almost unbearable.  It’s hard to imagine that this is designed for us from the Lord.  But it must be.

 

This morning, while sorrow attacked me, the Lord inspired me several sentences, I quickly wrote it down…

“Focus on what I have, the blessings from the Lord.

Do not focus on what I lost, which can and will (already) become the blessings of many.

Do not doubt the wisdom of the Lord.

It will be fair and square at the end...”

 

It gave me great comfort.  Praise the Lord.
