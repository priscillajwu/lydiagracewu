April 5, 2006, Wednesday

Our heavenly father has answered our prayer…

Two days ago, Lydia felt terrible. We have been praying in despair…

The new medications which treat Lydia’s sore throat and stomachache seem to be effective. Lydia is feeling better today. Praise the Lord for He has full of grace.

Lydia went with her sisters to tennis lesson. She even played for a few minutes. It’s amazing.

Thanks to our Lord. May His name be glorified.
