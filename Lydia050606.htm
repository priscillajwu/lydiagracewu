Dear Friends,

            Today, May 6th, Lydia participated in a musical called Honk Jr (The Ugly Duckling). She made me very proud. Once at school, I had written her a letter, which said:

Dear Lydia,

            I hope you are feeling better. I don’t have much to say because you are my sister. But I hope "I love you" covers it all. You’re always in my heart.

Your sister,

Priscilla

She made me very proud because, although she is so sick, she did very well. Everyone was saying good job to Lydia, and watching her, I felt pride well up in my heart. The cast also put together a huge basket of ducks as congratulations to her hard work and for her doing the musical.

I smiled as in my heart, I thought, "Congratulations, Lydia! I love you!"

Love,

Priscilla
