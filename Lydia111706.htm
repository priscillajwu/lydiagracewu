Thanks everyone for praying my eyes’ problem. Two - three weeks ago, there was a big lump on my left eye, and my left eye bag was swollen and was painful. Doctor called it was Blepharitis with Stye. I was treated with antibiotic ointment, heat press, and also took antibiotic medicine twice a day for 10 days by mouth. The worse of all was I was not allowed to wear my contact lens. Wednesday, I went back to see the doctor for the follow up appointment. The Stye (big lump) is almost gone. But I still need to continue treating my Blepharitis for at least 2 – 4 weeks.
