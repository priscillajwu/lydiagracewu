August 2, 2006, Wednesday

Lydia got up this morning, as usual, with terrible headache and nausea. After feeding her many pain and anti-nauseous medicines, she kind of felt better, and went ahead to play with her siblings.

Only couple hours later, her headache grew so severe that she could only cry and scream (it’s really worse than ever). Mom tried to calm her down. Lydia said she could not stand anymore and about to call 911 by herself to get the ambulance to get her to the hospital. Very concerned, mom called the hospital, and was told to rush to the hospital.

In the hospital, a brain CT scan, and X-ray on her belly were quickly performed. During those hours, again, difficult and hard to endure.  The experience of February 6th came to my mind again.  Actually, I would not think it’s another tumor (mom afraid so). We have been doing high dose radiation therapy and chemotherapy. But why, why is she still having so much headaches.

Hours later, CT scan and X-ray results came back normal. Which is good. Thank God. But then, what, what is causing her headache.

By the time I came home late in the night, mom and Lydia were still in the hospital, closely monitored.

Lydia will have several doctors scheduled to see her tomorrow. We pray for God’s healing, pray for the doctors able to find the cause of her headache and be able to treat her.

One more thing, we were planning to attend baseball game tonight, a free invitation, courtesy of Cincinnati Reds for the entire family (for brain cancer patient family). Everybody was so excited since several days ago. However, we had to give it up since Lydia is in the hospital under the emergency care.

A day like today is very difficult to live. We can only come before the Lord and ask for his mercy…
