May 30, 2006, Tuesday

Lydia went to swim this afternoon.  She swam for about a few laps.  Then, she was very tired.

Lydia ate better today.  The medication seems to be working.  We tried to find her pattern of eating.  Maybe the exercise would help.

Lydia also spent many hours today doing her homework.  This was the longest hours she ever spent per day since she got sick.  She was trying very hard to make up the school works she missed.

We pray for Lydia to have enough the strength and wisdom to do the work.  Whatever she could do, it’s all God’s mercy and grace.  God’s name should be glorified.
