Sep. 27, 2006

It’s so hard to live everyday without thinking of Lydia. Whenever I think about her, I cry. So… it is just really hard not to cry.

I have thought about hundred of reasons to help me think positively. All the positive thoughts are supposed to help me to think of her without tears.

But, it’s impossible, I just miss her too much.

When is this going to end ?!

Actually, the song of "Untitled Hymn (Come to Jesus)" is also my favorite song. I said that to the entire family including Lydia I would like to use this hymn for my OWN memorial service. I remembered when I said that Lydia cried and said,

"Mom, you took care of me so much while I am sick, if you die, I will cry really badly."

But, how would I ever expect that it ended up I am the one who sang this song to remember her in her memorial service.

I miss her talking so much. Everyday, she would come home with a lot to talk about. Especially, she would talk non-stop after she has been on a field trip. She would tell me everything happened that day. But, not this time. I won’t be able to know what’s happening to her now. I have to wait until I see her again in heaven to catch up with her. It is just way too long to wait.

God, please help me to live the life which YOU has planned for me! Help me to continue Lydia’s mission on earth, although I really wish to see her and my heavenly Father as soon as possible.

 

Jean
