September 4, 2007, Tuesday

Mom and dad were invited to a radio station recording today.  A talk station 55KRC, 550 AM, “Courageous Living” sponsored by Matthew 25 ministries.

We were so amazed by the hosts and producers of the program that they did so much homework.  They read all the entries in the web site and already knew a lot about us before the recording.  We were so honored to be able to share Lydia’s story, and have the opportunity to tell many potential radio listeners.

Throughout the recording, we were able to talk about Lydia in a great detail.  We felt so good, extremely comforted by the opportunity to talk about Lydia freely.

Many thanks to the dear sisters in Christ made the connection for us.  And the production teams for doing such a great job and their hospitality.

And praise the Lord, it’s awesome to be able to share Lydia’s story to inspire many people through the radio and to encourage people who are also facing the terrible adversity in life…

 

Please tune in to 55KRC AM 550, 9 PM, Sunday, September 9th.
