November 28, 2006, Tuesday

Mom and dad went to a nearby church for the grieving support group we found out recently.

Upon arrival, we realized we were the only people there other than the group leader.  So, we did not get to know any other parents who are grieving.  Not able to fellowship with others, we watched a video about grieving for the death of the child.

After the program, we realized that we have known a lot of material covered in the video already.  Because the Scriptures are truly the best source and help for the grieving.  The comfort, the promise, the loving, the consoling, the understanding, the question, the support … deep in our souls, all can be found in the Scriptures.

Praise the Lord, the almighty consoler.

 

Thank God for giving us the faith.  It’s so precious.  The Lord Himself is the ultimate comfort, nothing else can compare.

Thank God for preparing us, which took many years, for this tremendous pain and sorrow…

 

Thank God for the peace…
