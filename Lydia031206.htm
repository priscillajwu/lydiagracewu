March 12, 06

My dear prayer heroes,

 

After absent from school for five weeks, Lydia got homework from in-home tutor.  She was really excited.  She put several folders together, marked them with subjects, and started to work on them right away.  You can see the joy from her face.  Very soon, she felt tired and stressed.  “It’s OK, take it easy.” I told her.

By the end of weekend, she said she has done a lot already, but could not finish all of them.  Then, we realized, she thought she needed to finish everything before Monday.  “No, it’s for the entire week, due next Friday.” Mom said.  Then, Lydia is relieved.

Lydia had a dentist appointment on Saturday morning.  She had a cavity needed to be refilled.  (Her teeth health is another thing we need to watch during the coming therapies.)  When dentist was going to put numbing medicine on her gum.  She said she did not need it.  The pain on the tooth compared with what she has been through recently is really small.  Besides, it took less time, and she did not need to wait for numbness to go away before she could start eating.  She was right.

Lydia has a prayer request,

“My head still hurts, even though I don’t have an idea why.  Please pray that it won’t be a problem.”

According to the doctor, she should not feel head pain after so many days after surgery.  We’ll keep watching.  If it continues, then we will need a CT scan.

Lydia will have another X-ray positioning this Tuesday.  Her radiation therapy starts this Wednesday.

 

In Him,

Simon
