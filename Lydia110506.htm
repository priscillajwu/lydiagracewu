November 5, 2006, Sunday

Mom took girls to go clothes shopping this afternoon, while I was in the church meeting in the afternoon.

For years, once in a while, mom would take all four girls for the clothes shopping.  They wore out the clothes very quickly.  They generally had a lot of fun among five of them.

“How could you do it?”  I was curious and asked mom at night, “Didn’t you feel sad while shopping?  How could you do it?”

I am sure mom would think of Lydia with a lot of pain, while shopping, since Lydia was really good at helping them pick the clothes and always gave a lot of good suggestions.

“What do you think?  Of course, it’s very difficult.”  Mom answered, almost choked.

“Wow, I admired you.” I told her. “I would not be able to do it.”

 

Mom has not been well these several days.  Her eyes hurt, her head hurts, and with abdominal pain constantly.

 

“Why you and daddy were not very healthy recently?”  One of girls asked.

I think 2 Corinthians 12:7-10 gave a very good answer.
