May 31, 2006, Wednesday

Lydia received a gift of a huge prayer blanket, from a loving aunt in Christ.  It’s a quilt embroidered with many small pieces with quotes and prayers from many people.  In the center, John 3:16, Lydia’s favorite verse was sewed with four colors, blue, green, red, and purple.  It’s almost too precious for us to receive.  Looking at the blanket, this has to be the love from our Lord.  Lydia is going to bring it to the hospital during her chemo treatment.

Mom agreed that Lydia could order a swim team spirit wear.  When they ordered a few days ago, Lydia was told she might have to wait until the end of June.  Lydia was quite disappointed.  (Nowadays, her feeling was sometimes very fragile.)

Today, surprisingly, Lydia got the sweatshirt, because somebody cancelled the order. It was exactly the right size and the color.  Lydia was real happy.

“God must be very good to you.”  The lady said.

Yes, indeed, although Lydia has cancer, we know God is good to her, and God is always here with us.

There are good times; there are bad times. (Ecclesiastes 7:14)  It’s difficult to live a life with a lot of ups and downs.  But thank God He gave us the faith.
